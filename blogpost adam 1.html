Color Run

On the third of June, My friends and I decided to embrace a new adventure, the colour run event. As I wore my black jersey, excitement filled the air. With each stride, a burst of vivid hues engulfed me, representing the joy of youth and camaraderie. Amidst the laughter and paint-splashed frenzy, I felt a sense of unity with my fellow runners. As the colour run concluded, my heart brimmed with euphoria, knowing I had not only completed a run but also woven unforgettable memories of togetherness and fun. It was a marathon of a 4km run and I won fifth place. My friends and I had so much fun that day.
